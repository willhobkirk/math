Will Hobkirk Website

Hello! I am Will Hobkirk, a PhD candidate at The University of Sydney.

My supervisor is Jonathan Spreer and my auxiliary supervisor is Stephan Tillman.

My research focus is in Algebraic and Geometric Topology.

Below is a model of a 3-Folded surface of maximal Euler characteristic embedded in L(9,1). In the image you see the intersection of the 3-Folded surface with one of the two solid tori of the genus 1 Heegard decomposition of L(9,1). The intersection of this 3-Folded surface with the other solid torus is a single meridional disk.
